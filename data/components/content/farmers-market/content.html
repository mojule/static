Ashburton Farmers Market

Crozier’s Free Range Turkeys have a stall at the Ashburton Farmers Market (corner of West and Havelock Streets) each Saturday morning, from the end of September through to the end of April. You will also find other stalls selling locally sourced and produced products, like honey, cakes, breads, free range eggs, vegetables, fruits and free range pork products.

We have some turkey products that you can only find at the Ashburton Farmers Market – another reason for you to come and visit us there! Check them out:
